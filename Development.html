CRD(Carbon Reducing Device)

Dedicated to the environment

Cars as well as other vehicles demand so much that the Earth has to provide enough to sustain them for the use of us humans. The extent of this leads to great damage on Earth, and takes up valuable time for cleaning and manufacturing the type of fuel as a few examples.

Electric cars are useful, yet demand huge amounts of electricity. Hydrogen cars, those need certain refined fuel in order to run properly. What we need is a device that gives back to the Earth, not just taking away from it. This will rely on a certain solution(lithium hydroxide) in order to achieve our goals. There will be no other requirement aside from the product and the given solution for a greater simplicity.
